Title of the movie Power
Producer Courtney A. Kemp
Leading actor Omari Hardwick
Leading actress Lela Loren
Rating PG
Brief description Power tells the story of James St. Patrick (Omari Hardwick), an intelligent, ruthless drug dealer who has the alias of "Ghost". He wishes to leave the criminal world to pursue legitimate business interests as a nightclub owner. St. Patrick aims to balance those two lives, while also avoiding police capture, trying to navigate his crumbling marriage and manage shifting economic alliances.
