In general, we should all take care of our environment as Robert Swan said it "The greatest threat to our planet is the belief that someone else will save it!", because we all depend on it in every aspect of our lives. We have to start it by ourselves and try to explain it to our families, friends, neighbours, etc. A good environment has a good impact on our countries too, in different domains. For example for the economic growth, for the good health of the citizens, for the wellbeing of the animals, etc.
